Pay As You Feel....

Why The Wonky Carrot Cafe?

The Wonky Carrot Cafe is a pay as you feel cafe. We offer a pay as you feel service and only ask for you to pay how much you feel the food isz and juices with fruit and veg that isnt bought by wholesalers. There is nothing wrong with the produce only that due to that fact of the shape and colour the public are less likely to buy it therefore its usually left on the shelfs wasted.

The Local Community

We want to support out local community as much as possible so as well as offering the pay as you feel scheme to help, we also want to support small local businesses! We sell local businesses products and any profit that is made we pump back into the business so that we can offer more sandwiches and gice more veg boxes at the end of the day!

Here 30 Mins Before We Close?

At the Wonky Carrot Cafe we want to limit as much waste as possible. Thats why we offer free veg boxes! All fruit and veg that isnt used up by the end of the day we divide it all up into cardboard veg boxes for you family to enjoy for dinner! However, this does come on a first come first serve basis so be quick as our boxes always go by the end of the day!
